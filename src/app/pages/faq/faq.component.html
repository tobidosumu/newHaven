Haven is a charity web app written in Angular. It is a platform where charity organisations and as well as individuals can seek for monetary, and material support from registered users on the app.

It is a platform where charity organisations and as well as individuals can seek for monetary, and material support from registered users on the app.

Click on Donate button on the Header then you would be directed to the categories section which houses various charities you can make a donation to.

Click on Create button on the Header then you would be directed to the page to input details about your Charity or the cause you want to promote.

Click on the moon icon on the header to switch to Dark mode.
